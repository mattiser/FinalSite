The aim of Home-In-A-Box is to provide customers with an affordable, easy and custom tiny house experience while still preserving the joy of building it yourself - even with little to no experience. Our staff are trained in order to provide the best advice and guidance in designing and constructing the optimal home for the customer, while still preserving convenience and safety in all manners of construction. The base Home-In-A-Box models contain most of the parts necessary to begin the building of your very own tiny house - minus materials like lumber, which are costly to ship and can be easily bought from your neighborhood hardware store. Each kit also contains detailed, step-by-step instructions on the construction of your custom home, and our employees are always on call to answer any questions you might have.
